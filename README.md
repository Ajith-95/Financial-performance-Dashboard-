###Financial-performance-Dashboard-

This README provides business-focused analysis, key insights, and metrics definitions for the dashboard included in this repository. The goal is to help hiring managers, recruiters, and collaborators understand analytical thinking, KPI design, and impact-driven approach as a Business Analyst.

#Objective;

To evaluate financial health, track revenue trends, analyze cost structures, and support strategic decisionâ€‘making.

#Key Metrics & KPIs:

Total Revenue & Revenue Growth %

Operating Expenses (OPEX) & Cost Trend %

Gross Margin & Operating Margin

Revenue vs Budget Variance

Profit Contribution by Department

Cash Flow Stability Indicators

#Insights Provided:

Month-over-month revenue growth identified seasonal performance cycles.

Department-level profitability comparison surfaced underperforming units.

Variance analysis helped identify overspending in marketing and operations.

Margin trends indicated opportunities for cost optimization.

#Business Impact:

Supports forecasting and financial planning.

Helps leadership identify high-impact cost savings.

Improves budgeting accuracy through historical trend visibility.

#How to Use These Dashboards:

Open the .twb / .twbx files using Tableau Desktop or Tableau Public.

Review metrics & insights listed above while exploring the dashboard.

Use the dashboards to extract business insights and re-run analysis with updated data.
